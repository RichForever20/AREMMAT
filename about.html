Our Story

AREMMAT SOLUTION LIMITED is a rapidly expanding alternative-energy company based in Nigeria's Capital City. They boast a team of skilled Engineers, Expatriates, Electricians, and Installers trained to deliver solar solutions meeting global standards. With expertise in meeting diverse energy requirements, they cater to the industry's demanding needs.

Aremmat Solution Limited has handled several solar projects including generation and distribution of solar energy to homes, schools, corporate offices, and government agencies across Nigeria and gradually extending to other countries in West Africa.

Agbuna Mathew O.

Chief Executive Officer

AREMMAT SOLUTION LIMITED

Matthew is a graduate of Electrical Electronics HND from Lagos State Polytechnic. He has contributed largely to the growth of the following organizations namely the Nigeria Wire Industry (NIWI) in the year 1999, as an Electrical Technician.

He later join DISC Engineering Co as an Electrical Technician in 2003, thereafter he move on to pursue his career as an electrical engineer at Osun state college of Technology Esa-Oke Osun State where he obtains his OND IN 2007, before joining Drug Field Pharmaceutical Limited in Electrical Technician. In 2008 he joined New- lord Nigeria Limited as a renewable Engineer, where he trained as an Installer/service & Sales Engineer, this gave him more edge in his career. In 2015, he joined Powercell Limited one of the leading renewable companies in Nigeria presently as a service engineer and rose to the role of Deputy Manager, Field operations manager.

With his wealth of knowledge in the field of engineering and 21 years of experience, he has shown his capacity and capability in the field. Matthew is a certified ABB engineer from ABB University; he also had a certification from SMA Germany, Huawei certified engineer, and Ablerex certified UPS commissioning expert. He also obtained a certificate in Safety in the workplace from the Hybrid group. As an Electrical Engineer, He has led his team in the installation of numerous solar installations such as the 10KW solutions for SCB branches, another 10KW solutions at Kelm Engineering, and other numerous UPS installations.
